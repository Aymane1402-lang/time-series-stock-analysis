# 📈 Time Series Stock Price Analysis with Python

## Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Time Series Analysis** of historical stock market data using Python. The objective is to explore stock price behavior, identify market trends, analyze trading activity, and compare the performance of multiple companies through statistical analysis and data visualization.

The notebook demonstrates essential time series techniques commonly used in financial analytics, providing insights into price movements, volatility, and long-term market behavior.

---

## Objectives

- Perform exploratory analysis on historical stock price data.
- Analyze stock price trends over time.
- Calculate moving averages to identify long-term patterns.
- Measure daily percentage returns.
- Detect significant price fluctuations.
- Compare the performance of multiple companies.
- Analyze trading volume over different time periods.
- Apply time series resampling techniques for weekly and monthly analysis.

---

The dataset contains daily historical stock market data, including:

## Key Insights

- Historical stock prices reveal both long-term growth trends and short-term volatility.
- Moving averages effectively smooth price fluctuations and highlight market direction.
- Daily returns help identify periods of unusually high market movement.
- Trading volume varies across companies and over time, reflecting changes in market activity.
- Comparing multiple stocks provides a broader understanding of relative performance.
